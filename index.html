Slytherin vs. Gryffindor

gryffindor

1. Gryffindors will never cheat! 2. They will always be humble 3. They will defend their friends 4. They take the long way to accomplish something if they think it's "right"

1. The ends justify the means 2. They will die for their family. 3. They always win 4. They sneak into Hogwarts through secret passages 5. They both produce great and powerful wizards

slytherin

1. Slytherins will cheat if they think the end is worth it 2. They will NOT always be humble 3. They will sometimes go behind their friends back 4. They will take shortcuts just to complete a task
